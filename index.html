Rules I live by when creating breakfast:

HEALTHY FOOD CAN BE SWEET
Many of you might think healthy food as bland, but when using plenty of fruit and natural sweet flavors, such as maple syrup or honey, nutritious food can be delictably sweet.

WHOLE FOODS ARE ESSENTIAL
The key to having a healthy diet isn't "low calories" or "high protein"; it's having a variety of nutritious whole foods (foods that are minimally processed such as oats, fruit, and more).

LET FOOD BE YOUR CANVAS
Food can be art. It doesn't have to be boring. Using many different fruits, toppings, spices, and more, often allows you to create an aesthetically-pleasing display of food if you choose to do so.

MAKE SURE TO ENJOY YOUR FOOD
If you don't like or enjoy the food that you're eating, you're not going to want to eat it again.
